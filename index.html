About me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher in the System Security group at Eurecom under the supervision of Davide Balzarotti. I received my PhD from Politecnico di Milano where I worked in the [NECSTLab] under the supervision of Stefano Zanero and Federico Maggi. I received my Laurea Magistrale in Software and Digital Systems, and Laurea from Politecnico di Torino.

As a Marie-Skłodowska Curie alumni, I’ve been an exchange student at UC Santa Barbara’ SecLab](). At the end of my PhD I also had a chance to work as an engineering intern in Qualcomm’ Product Security group under the supervision of Pouyan Sepehrdad.

I love teaching: worked as TA in basic programming, and computer security courses, and as a freelance consultant on different projects, includin teaching of malware analysis, mobile and windows reverse engineering for the Consorzio Interuniversitario Nazionale per l’Informatica, and national, and international clients of Italian security firms Secure Network, and Shorr Kan.
